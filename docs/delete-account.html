FinTrack – Personal Finance Manager

Option 1 — Delete from within the App (Recommended)

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the FinTrack app:

  1. Open the FinTrack app on your device
  2. Tap the Settings icon (bottom navigation or profile menu)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted
⚠️ This will permanently delete your account and all your financial data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us. We will process your request within 30 days.

  • Send an email to: support@miser.financeapp
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address associated with your FinTrack account
Email Us to Delete Account

What Data Gets Deleted?

  • ✅ Your Google/Apple account link (authentication)
  • ✅ All expense, income, and budget records
  • ✅ All goals, wallets, and settings
  • ✅ All cloud-synced data from Firebase/Firestore

Data that may be retained: Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot be linked back to you (retained for up to 90 days).
