Privacy Policy

FinTrack – Personal Finance Manager

1. Introduction

Welcome to FinTrack ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (com.miser.financeapp). By using FinTrack, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic profile data (name, email address, and profile picture) provided by those services.
  • Financial Data: Expense records, income entries, budget limits, and categories that you manually enter into the app.
  • Device Information: Basic device identifiers and operating system version, used to improve app stability.
  • Cloud Sync Data: If you enable sync, your financial records are stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) to enable cross-device access.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate FinTrack's core features (expense tracking, budgeting, analytics).
  • To sync your data across devices when you are signed in.
  • To send optional in-app notifications and reminders for recurring expenses.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using Hive (an encrypted local database). If you use cloud sync, data is stored securely in Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We follow best practices to protect your financial information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

FinTrack uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication, cloud storage
  • Google Sign-In – Social login
  • Sign in with Apple – Social login

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app's Settings screen. Upon deletion, all cloud-synced data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. Locally stored data is deleted immediately when you uninstall the app.

7. Children's Privacy

FinTrack is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of data processing for non-essential purposes.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date below. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
